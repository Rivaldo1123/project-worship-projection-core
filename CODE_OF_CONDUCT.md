# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, welcoming, and productive for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, denomination, geography, or role.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing technical concerns directly.
- Give and accept feedback professionally.
- Respect privacy, licensing, and the operational realities of volunteer-led organizations.
- Focus discussion on improving the project and serving its users.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate disruption, publication of private information, and repeated bad-faith conduct are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer using the contact information on the maintainer's GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed as promptly and confidentially as practical.
